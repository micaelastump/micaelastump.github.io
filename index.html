    About

    Sprouting from a simple Christmas gift, the Bloody Stump Brewery has slowly taking over more and more of the Stump family lifesyle. What began as a hobby has resulted in renovating the man cave into a brewery, embroidered sweatshirts, and hoarding bottles so no one can try them. So if you would like to try a bottle of deliscious and expertly made Bloody Stump beer...too bad.
